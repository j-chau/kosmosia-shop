Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I place an order?

    1. Click on "Add to Cart" to add the items you want to your shopping cart.
      After submitting the checkout form, you will be contacted by @kosmosia.shop on instagram to confirm payment options.

      Or, DM @kosmosia.shop directly on instagram.


      Orders processed through this website are nonbinding

  2. What payment method do you take?

    1. Paypal for friends and family only to kosmosia.shop@gmail.com;
      or e-transfer (email to be sent upon placing order)

  3. Do you ship worldwide?

    1. Yes I do! All mail is stamped (no tracking), and are shipped by Canada Post

      The following table applies for orders that are less than 4 items

      Canada $3.00 2-5 business days
      USA $5.00 7-14 business days
      International $8.00 14-30 business days

      For orders that are 5-7 items, an extra $1 USD will be charged
      For orders over 7 items, the final shipping price will be discussed after placing the order

      Finch Station
